# Code of Conduct

Faultline follows a simple standard: be professional, constructive, and
respectful. The project is for building useful local developer tooling, and
maintainers may moderate behavior that makes technical collaboration harder.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, sustained disruption,
personal attacks, publishing private information, and abusive or discriminatory
language.

Report conduct concerns to conduct@faultline.dev. Maintainers may remove
content, close threads, restrict participation, or take other reasonable action
to keep the project healthy.
